# chess3.xyz Figma Stylesheet

## Foundations
### Colors
- Background: #FCFBF8
- Surface: #FFFFFF
- Primary Text: #0F1114
- Muted Text: #5C6168
- Accent (dark gold): #B08B4F
- Border/Grid: #EFE8DA (4–6% opacity)
- Success: #2E7D32
- Warning: #A66A2A
- Error: #B23A3A

### Typography
- Title (handwritten): Caveat
  - H1 56/64, 600
  - H2 40/48, 600
  - H3 28/36, 600
- Label (caps): Space Grotesk
  - Label L 12/16, 600, tracking +0.08em
  - Label M 14/18, 600, tracking +0.06em
- Body: Inter
  - Body L 18/28, 400
  - Body M 16/24, 400
  - Body S 14/20, 400
  - Emphasis 16/24, 600

### Spacing + Layout
- 8pt spacing system
- Max content width: 1120px
- Grid background: 64px squares at 4% opacity (#EFE8DA)
- Corner radius: 8px (cards), 12px (modals), 999px (chips)

### Effects
- Card shadow: 0 6px 20px rgba(15,17,20,0.06)

### Cards
