# Brand Guidelines

## Narrative
- Instant Arena now; Rewarded Mastery later.
- Quiet‑premium, classical, gamified.

## Color Tokens
- Background: #0B0D10
- Surface: #12151A
- Primary Text: #E4DED4
- Muted Text: #A7A094
- Gold (buttons): #B08B4F
- Gold (text): #C7A96B
- Border/Grid: #1A1E24 (4–6% opacity)

## Typography
- Handwritten: Caveat 600
- Caps: Space Grotesk 600
- Body: Inter 400/600

## Buttons
- Size: 12px text, 6x10 padding
- Radius: 4px
- Primary: Gold
- Light: Grey (#E2DDD3)

## Voice
- Short, deliberate sentences.
- No hype; emphasize speed, fairness, reward loops.
