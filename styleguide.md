# Styleguide

## Brand
- Narrative: Instant Arena now; Rewarded Mastery later.
- Tone: quiet‑premium, classical, gamified.
- Style: deep black base; ultra‑clean; subtle grid.

## Color Tokens
- Background: #0B0D10
- Surface: #12151A
- Primary Text: #E4DED4
- Muted Text: #A7A094
- Gold (buttons): #B08B4F
- Gold (text): #C7A96B
- Border/Grid: #1A1E24 (4–6% opacity)
- Success: #2E7D32
- Warning: #A66A2A
- Error: #B23A3A

## Typography
- Titles (handwritten): Caveat
  - H1 56/64, 600
  - H2 40/48, 600
  - H3 28/36, 600
- Labels (caps): Space Grotesk
  - 12/16, 600, tracking +0.08em
  - 14/18, 600, tracking +0.06em
- Body: Inter
  - 18/28, 400
  - 16/24, 400
  - 14/20, 400
  - Emphasis 16/24, 600

## Layout
- 8pt spacing system
- Max content width: 1120px
- Grid background: 64px squares at 4% opacity (#1A1E24)
- Corner radius: 6px cards, 8px modals, 999px chips
- Card shadow: 0 10px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.35)

## Component Rules
- Buttons: compact (12px text, 6x10 padding), radius 4px
- Primary button: BG #B08B4F, text #0B0D10, hover #9C7A45, focus ring #B08B4F 2px
- Secondary button: BG #15181E, border #2A2F38, text #E4DED4, hover BG #1C2027
- Light button: BG #E2DDD3, border #D3CFC6, text #14161A
- Inputs: border #1A1E24, placeholder #8A8F96, focus ring #B08B4F
- Badges: BG #15171D, border #2A2F38, text #C7A96B, uppercase labels
